My Todo-LIST

Input Your Task: Enter the task you wish to add to your ToDo list. This could be anything from a reminder to call a friend, a deadline for a project, or any other task you need to remember.

Set a Date: After entering your task, input the date when you plan to complete the task.

Add the Task: Once you've entered the task and set a date, select the 'Add' option to add your task to the list.

Delete a Task: If you need to remove a task from your list, you can do so by selecting the 'Delete' option and choosing the task you wish to remove.
